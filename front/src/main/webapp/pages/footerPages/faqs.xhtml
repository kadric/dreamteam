1.Prohibited items in checked baggage

Passengers may not carry these following items, or be placed in baggage:

Any weapons
Any sharp items
Any blunt instruments
Any explosive or flammable items
Any chemicals or toxic substances
Any liquids over 30ml, including drinks bought outside of the airport.

2.I have forgotten/lost my boarding pass

Once a passenger has checked in online the boarding pass can be reprinted up to 2 hours before the scheduled flight departure time. Click here to reprint your boarding pass

Passengers who arrive at the airport who have lost/forgotten their printed online boarding passes will be required to pay a Boarding Card Reissue Fee in order to receive a new boarding pass at the airport. This facility is available up to 40 minutes prior to the scheduled flight departure.

3.I have not received my Dzenky Air email itinerary - do I have a booking?

Occasionally due to delays with our payment processing system, bookings may not be confirmed and an email confirmation will not be sent.

If this occurs our booking system attempts to process the payment again and if authorisation is not received from the bank the flights are then automatically cancelled and no confirmation email travel itinerary is sent.
