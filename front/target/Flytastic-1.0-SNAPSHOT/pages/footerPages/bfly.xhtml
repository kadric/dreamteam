Before flying with us

Baggage


When flying with Dzenky air, you are allowed to have one bag in the cabin, with a max weight of 5kg. You bag may not exceed the measurements of 100cm x 70cm x 50cm. You are also allowed to have baggage(max 4 bags) with a total max weight of 15kg. Please note that if you exceed these weights&measurements, you will not be allowed to enter your bag/baggage into the airplane. You can not buy extra baggage space on our flights.

Allergy


We work hard to meet our customers demand. We are trying to keep our planes allery safe. If you have any allergies, contact us before flying to let you know if you can safely travel with us.

Food


We do not serve any food on our flights. We do serve snacks and drinks onboard our flights. You may not bring any food&drinks on our planes, unless you bought it from the airport.

Board ticket&passport


To fly with us, you will need to bring you boarding ticket. You will recieve the boarding ticket after booking your ticket. You will also need to bring your passport. You will not be able to fly with us if you dont have your boardin ticket and/or your passport.
